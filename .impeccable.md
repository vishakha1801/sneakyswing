## Design Context

### Users
Primarily students — recreational to serious golfers trying to improve their swing. Coaches are secondary. They find this page from the App Store or referrals, often on mobile after a round. The site is a landing page for the iOS app.

### Brand Personality
Beautiful and effective. A little 8-bit feel — precise, structured, not kiddish. Professional but not corporate. Earnest rather than ironic.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme (cream background, forest green accent, near-black text). The 8-bit quality should come through in design details — pixel-grid accents, crisp geometric precision in UI elements, square dots as indicators — NOT from literal pixel fonts. Typography should be elegant and refined, with the 8-bit character carried by the body/label font and decorative details. Anti-references: bright/neon, childish gaming aesthetics, clunky dashboard UIs, overly serious corporate golf.

### Design Principles
1. Elegance first, 8-bit as a whisper — the retro quality shows in structure and details, not in color or typography loudness
2. Students before coaches — the emotional hook is the golfer's improvement journey
3. Beautiful italic serif headings + geometric-precise body sans = the core typographic tension
4. Precision and warmth together — crisp edges and warm cream, exact and inviting
5. Never kiddish — every design choice should feel like it belongs in a premium sports product
